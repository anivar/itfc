Overview

IT for Change received a total of Rs 16.7 million during the year, of which Rs. 4 million was received from the Ford Foundation, New York for the project 'Deepening Democracy', Rs 9.1 million from International Development Research Centre-Canada for the projects 'Making Local Governance Work for Women: Exploring New Institutional Possiblities' and 'Gender and Citizenship in the Information Society', Rs 2.3 million from UN Women- New York for the project 'Making Womens Voices and Votes Count', Rs 0.8 million was received from Cognizant Foundation- India and UNESCO- India for the project 'Teachers Communities Of Learning'.

Ongoing / upcoming projects for 2013-14 IT for Change projects for 2013-14 include the following

  1. Making Local Governance Work for Women: Exploring New Institutional Possiblities”, International Development Research Centre-Canada
  2. Making Womens Voices and Votes Count, UN Women- New York
  3. "Subject Teacher Forum" and Karnataka Open Education Resources, Central Square Foundation, UNICEF/ Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan,(RMSA),
  4. Kalike, Sir Ratan Tata Trust
  5. Teachers Communities Of Learning, Cognizant Foundation- India (CF) and Commonwealth Educational Media Centre for Asia, (CEMCA)
  6. Crowd Sourcing World Map of UNESCO Point of Interests, UNESCO
